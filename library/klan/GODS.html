GODS

 

You are sleeping...

I cannot fall asleep... I must not sleep... I am reviewing... reviewing around, reviewing on you thru drafty semidarkness. Where are you? ...in what worlds the stars are presently lighted for you? What sky presently reflects you? You lie beside, but you are so far. And in ditto time so close that I can consider, as you are changed in one`s dreams. I am changing you, you see my own dreams...

...Gekata, goddess of the Moon, crossroads and half-night road... it awaits the solitary of wanderer, on its trouble started the way in her hour...

...Lilith, primitive, haughty and ruthlessly beautiful spirit of eternal femininity...

You sleep...

I cannot fall asleep... I am reviewing on you... I am falling in you and I am crashing on a million of splinters... but can not budge... cannot break your dreamЕ after all, I must be your dream...

I think about you, about myself, about the Way, about the road ahead, which lies On The Dark Side.

My element Ц Fire...

Your Ц Air...

I am burning salamander, appearing and in flame a candle, and in solar winds.

You are sleeping... you are so far away... but you will not be in solitude in the dream any longer... I will fall into them not even having asked your permits... sometimes we shall go beside, sometimes nothing not to give my presence, but I don`t care, i shall be there...

Hereafter we shall go together for long, go and search for our Guild On the Dark Side...

But, I.. I already do not see any dreams for a long time. But I will show you my daydream... someday...
